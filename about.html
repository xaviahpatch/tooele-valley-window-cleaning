Who We Are

A Local Business Built on Hard Work and Community

Tooele Valley Window Cleaning started with a simple idea: the people of Tooele Valley deserve a window cleaning service that actually shows up on time, does the job right, and treats your home like their own.

What began as a one-person operation eight years ago has grown into a trusted name across the valley — from Tooele and Stansbury Park to Grantsville and beyond. We've built our reputation one clean window at a time, through honest work and genuine care for our customers and community.

We're a family-friendly business. That means we understand what it's like to have kids running around, pets underfoot, and a busy schedule that doesn't have room for hassle. We work quickly, cleanly, and respectfully — always leaving your home exactly as we found it, just with much better windows.

Our Commitment

We Treat Every Home Like Our Own

Every member of our team is trained, background-checked, and committed to the same standard of excellence we've upheld since day one. We wear shoe covers inside your home, clean up after ourselves, and never leave a mess behind.

We're fully licensed and insured in the state of Utah, so you can feel completely confident inviting us onto your property. Whether we're cleaning a second-story window or working on a large commercial building, we follow strict safety protocols to protect both our team and your property.

At the end of the day, we're not just a business — we're part of this community. We sponsor local events, support Tooele Valley families, and genuinely care about the place we call home.
